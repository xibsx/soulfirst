## What is a Founding Builder?

A Founding Builder is someone who contributed to SoulFirst in its earliest days — when the repository was nearly empty, the community didn't exist yet, and the vision was still just words on a page.

Their names are recorded here permanently. As SoulFirst grows, this page will not. It is reserved for those who came first.

## How to Become a Founding Builder

This window is open now, but it won't be open forever.

You qualify by doing any of the following **before the project reaches 100 stars**:

- Submit a pull request that gets merged
- Open an issue that meaningfully shapes the project's direction
- Contribute documentation, design, or research
- Build a module, adapter, or integration

There is no application. Show up, build, and you're in.

---

## Recognition

Founding Builders receive:

- **Permanent listing** on this page — your name stays here as long as SoulFirst exists
- **Founding Builder title** — recognized in the project as a founding member, with a dedicated badge displayed alongside your name
- **Early input rights** — your voice carries extra weight in architectural discussions during the founding phase
- **Credits in the first release notes** — when SoulFirst ships its first tagged release, Founding Builders are named
- **Priority access** — early access to new features, tools, and integrations before public release

## Why This Page Exists

Open source projects grow fast. In a year, there might be hundreds of contributors. In five years, maybe thousands.

But there will only ever be one group of people who showed up before the soul had a body.

This page is our way of saying:

**The people who build the fire deserve to be remembered — not just the people who warm their hands by it.**
